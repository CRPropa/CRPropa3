\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Nuclear decay of unstable nuclei. 

This module simulates the nuclear decay of unstable nuclei using data from Nu\+Dat2. All decay modes are considered\+: alpha, beta+-\/ and gamma decay, as well as proton-\/ and neutron dripping. The resulting non-\/hadronic secondary particles (e+, e-\/, neutrinos, gamma) can optionally be created.

For details on the preprocessing of the Nu\+Dat2 data refer to \char`\"{}\+CRPropa3-\/data/calc\+\_\+decay.\+py\char`\"{}.
