\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Multiple nuclei with a rigidity-\/dependent power-\/law spectrum. 

The power law is of the form\+: E$^\wedge$index, for energies in the interval \mbox{[}Emin, Z $\ast$ Rmax\mbox{]}.
