\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Deactivates the candidate below a minimum energy for specific particle Ids. 

This modules deactivates the candidate below a given minimum energy for specific particle types. In that case the property (\char`\"{}\+Deactivated\char`\"{}, module\+::description) is set. All particles whose minimum energies are not specified follow the more general min\+Energy\+Others condition.
