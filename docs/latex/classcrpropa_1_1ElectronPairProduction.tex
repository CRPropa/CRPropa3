\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Electron-\/pair production of charged nuclei with background photons. 

This module simulates electron-\/pair production as a continuous energy loss.~\newline
Several photon fields can be selected.~\newline
The production of secondary e+/e-\/ pairs and photons can by activated.~\newline
By default, the module limits the step size to 10\% of the energy loss length of the particle.
