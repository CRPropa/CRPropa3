\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Modulated magnetic field on a periodic grid. 

This class wraps a Grid3f to serve as a \mbox{\hyperlink{classcrpropa_1_1MagneticField}{Magnetic\+Field}}. The field is modulated on-\/the-\/fly with a Grid1f. The Grid3f and Grid1f do not need to share the same origin, spacing or size.
