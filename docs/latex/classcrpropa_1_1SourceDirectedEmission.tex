\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Directed emission from a source from the von-\/\+Mises-\/\+Fisher distribution. 

The emission from the source is generated following the von-\/\+Mises-\/\+Fisher distribution with mean direction mu and concentration parameter kappa. The sampling from the v\+MF distribution follows this document by Julian Straub\+: \href{http://people.csail.mit.edu/jstraub/download/straub2017vonMisesFisherInference.pdf}{\texttt{ http\+://people.\+csail.\+mit.\+edu/jstraub/download/straub2017von\+Mises\+Fisher\+Inference.\+pdf}} The emitted particles are assigned a weight so that the detected particles can be reweighted to an isotropic emission distribution instead of a v\+MF distribution. For details, see PoS (ICRC2019) 447.
