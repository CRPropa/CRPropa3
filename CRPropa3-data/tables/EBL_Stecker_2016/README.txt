Extragalactic background light model from Stecker et al. 2016, DOI:10.3847/0004-637X/827/1/6
"An Empirical Determination of the Intergalactic Background Light from UV to FIR Wavelengths Using FIR Deep Galaxy Surveys and the Gamma-ray Opacity of the Universe"

Files obtained from Floyd Stecker on Sep 14, 2016 corresponding to figures 5 and 6 of the publication.
The rows are log photon energy ranging from -2.84 to 1.14 spaced 0.01.
The columns are redshifts from 0 to 5 spaced 0.01.
The units are erg/Hz cm-3.
