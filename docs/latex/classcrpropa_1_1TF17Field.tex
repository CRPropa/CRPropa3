\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classcrpropa_1_1TF17Field}{TF17\+Field}} galactic magnetic field model. 

Implements the TF2017 galactic magnetic field model, consisting of large-\/scale regular disk and halo fields. The field is defined in the usual galactocentric coordinate system with the Galactic center at the origin, the x-\/axis pointing in the opposite direction of the Sun, and the z-\/axis pointing towards Galactic north. See\+: Terral, \mbox{\hyperlink{classcrpropa_1_1Ferriere}{Ferriere}} 2017 -\/ Constraints from Faraday rotation on the magnetic field structure in the galactic halo, DOI\+: 10.\+1051/0004-\/6361/201629572, ar\+Xiv\+:1611.\+10222
